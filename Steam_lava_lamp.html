Lava Lamp

MATERIALS

  • Baking Soda
  • Vinegar
  • Food Coloring
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Glass Jar

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Add a tablespoon of baking soda in the bottom of the jar.

2. Pour the vegetable oil in the jar until almost full.

3. Pour about a tablespoon vinegar into a small dish or a bottlecap.

4. Add a few drops of your favorite food coloring to the vinegar.

5. Pour the colored vinegar into the jar.

6. Watch the chemical reaction! You can even get a flashlight, to shine through the lava lamp while the blobs are bubbling!

HOW IT WORKS

The vinegar sinks to the bottom as it is denser than the vegetable oil. When it meets the baking soda in the bottom then starts to dissolve. As it dissolves it makes gas, carbon dioxide. Gas or air is lighter than oil so it floats to the top. The air bubbles bring some colored water with them to the top. When the air comes out of the colored water blob, the water gets heavy again and sinks. It does this over and over again until the baking soda is completely dissolved.
